In-lecture Examples

What is the concentration of ethanol in a solution that is 40% ethanol by mass and has a density of 0.945 g/mL?

An unknown compound contains only carbon, hydrogen, and oxygen atoms. When 5.00 grams of this substance is combusted in excess oxygen, 10.22 grams of carbon dioxide and 3.14 grams of water are produced. What is the empirical formula of the compound?
